User Journey and Features Design

Target users

- Swahili learners

Target users’ pain points:

- As an agglutinative language, it’s troublesome and time consuming for students to look up new words in dictionary. (Analyze-concatenate-look up manually for every word)

- It’s hard for students to find extra-curriculum reading themselves – difficult to find articles both on topics they are interested in and suitable for their current language proficiency.

Functions description

- Allow students to look words up with a simple click in the context

- Allow students to manage their vocabulary books

- Recommend new articles to students based on their reading history and new learnt words.
